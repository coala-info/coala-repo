## Description

Galaxy Workflow created on Galaxy-E european instance, ecology.usegalaxy.eu, related to the Galaxy training tutorial "[Sentinel 2 biodiversity](https://training.galaxyproject.org/training-material/topics/ecology/tutorials/species-distribution-modeling/tutorial.html)" .

This workflow allows to analyze remote sensing sentinel 2 satellites data to compute spectral indices such as the NDVI and visualizing biodiversity indicators
