You may start with the tutorial or dive into the documentation . Authors iCount is developed and supported by Tomaž Curk from the Bioinformatics Laboratory at the University of Ljubljana , Faculty of Computer and Information Science and in collaboration with the laboratory of Jernej Ule . The development started in late 2008 when Tomaž Curk and Gregor Rot wrote a first prototype of iCount. A lot has happend since then.
