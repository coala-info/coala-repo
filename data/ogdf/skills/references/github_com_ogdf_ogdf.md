OGDF is a self-contained C++ library for graph algorithms, in particular for (but not restricted to) automatic graph drawing. It offers sophisticated algorithms and data structures to use within your own applications or scientific projects. OGDF is developed and supported by Osnabrück University , University of Passau , University of Konstanz , TU Vienna , and TU Ilmenau .
