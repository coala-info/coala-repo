From: [Genome-wide identification and differential analysis of translational initiation](/articles/s41467-017-01981-8)

![Fig. 4](//media.springernature.com/full/springer-static/image/art%3A10.1038%2Fs41467-017-01981-8/MediaObjects/41467_2017_1981_Fig4_HTML.jpg)

Genome-wide identification and differential analysis of TIs. **a** rRibo-seq and LTM-based TI-seq RPF count profiles in HEK293 cell line for TUBA1B, suggesting a uORF being translated across a different reading frame (pink) from annotated one (skyblue). **b** The normalized QTI-seq RPF count profiles under normal condition and amino-acid deprivation for the longest isoform of *C1QBP*. The top enriched **c** biological processes and **d** cellular components, based on the GO enrichment analysis of the genes with significantly elevated translation initiation efficiency under amino-acid deprivation. The normalized QTI-seq RPF and RNA-seq count profiles under normal condition and amino-acid deprivation for genes encoding mitochondrial ribosomal proteins **e**
