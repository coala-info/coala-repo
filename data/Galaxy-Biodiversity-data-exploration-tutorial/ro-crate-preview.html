Research Object Crate for Biodiversity data exploration tutorial

Original URL: https://workflowhub.eu/workflows/656/ro_crate?version=1

Galaxy Workflow created on Galaxy-E european instance, ecology.usegalaxy.eu, related to the Galaxy training tutorial "[Biodiversity data exploration](https://training.galaxyproject.org/training-material/topics/ecology/tutorials/biodiversity-data-exploration/tutorial.html)" This workflow allows to explore biodiversity data looking at homoscedasticity, normality or collinearity of presences-absence or abundance data and at comparing beta diversity taking into account space, time and species components
