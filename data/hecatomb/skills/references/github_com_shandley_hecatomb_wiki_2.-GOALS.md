# 2. GOALS

[Jump to bottom](#wiki-pages-box)

Scott Handley edited this page Oct 21, 2020
·
[3 revisions](/shandley/hecatomb/wiki/2.-GOALS/_history)

There are a large number of steps required to ensure accurate and sensitive analysis of viral sequences from metagenomic data. These steps begin with rigorous quality control, proceed through taxonomic assignment and result in the production of several tables of information useful for virome analysis. There are generally two goals of hecatomb. First is to provide a robust workflow designed to provide the most-sensitive and most-specific taxonomic assignment of metagenomic sequences while maintaining accurate count information. The second is to provide a framework for combining these data with study sample data to enable researcher interrogation of their viromes in the context of their specific study.
