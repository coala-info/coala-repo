In addition, when possible, Hopla executes offline pedigree haplotyping through Merlin . Other than post-natal analyses, its all-inclusive output enables embryo selection during preimplantation genetic testing, ultimately intending birth of healthy children in affected families. The name 'Hopla' originates from being both a 'haplo' anagram and a popular kids' television show.
