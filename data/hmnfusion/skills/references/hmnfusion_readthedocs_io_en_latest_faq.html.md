## References[¶](#references "Link to this heading")

HmnFusion uses hg19 human genome.

## Bed files[¶](#bed-files "Link to this heading")

3 BED files are used by default or you can provide custom BED files:

* Lumpy. The original BED has been slightly modified [`(download)`](_downloads/08f03ad5507d6fb27e33d2c6886a967f/lumpy.bed)
* Genefuse. The original BED has been slightly modified [`(download)`](_downloads/6397ac9b0c8c500f3d16d9e416d00437/genefuse.bed)
* HmnFusion. Contains the location of the primers used in [10.1016/j.jmoldx.2022.07.004](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1525157822002185?via%3Dihub) [`(download)`](_downloads/e645cdb71df1711540840dbb8abd94b5/hmnfusion.bed)

If you want to use your own design, and you have some questions, feel free to open an [issue](https://github.com/guillaume-gricourt/HmnFusion/issues)
