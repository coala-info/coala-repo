# Tools and resources

**We develop a range of software and bioinformatic tools to support the life sciences across data management and analysis.**

---

These tools enable integration, analysis, and interpretation of large or complex datasets, such as those generated by multiple omics technologies. They can also accelerate knowledge discovery from biological data, for example, developing platforms for data-sharing and integration.

Our tools help you to implement FAIR principles - making data findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable - as well as enabling wider access to advanced data analysis systems.

Most of our tools and software have been created as part of research projects. While they may not be ‘off-the-shelf’ products, our talented developers can help to engineer bespoke solutions to your specific needs.

For more information on collaboration, licensing, or services, please visit the '[accessing our tools and software](https://www.earlham.ac.uk/accessing-our-tools-and-software "Accessing our tools and software")' page or speak to the [Business Development and Impact team](https://www.earlham.ac.uk/business-development-and-impact "Contact the Business Development and Impact team").

## All available tools

[## ALVIS

A tool to generate visualisations for a number of common long read/contig alignment analysis tasks.](/alvis)

[## BubbleParse

A tool for locating SNPs in non-reference organisms from sequence reads alone.](/bubbleparse)

[## Grassroots Genomics

The Grassroots Genomics platform consolidates data and analyses, facilitating consistent approaches to generating, processing and disseminating public wheat datasets.](/grassroots-genomics)

[## Kontaminant

A kmer-based screening and filtering tool for next-gen sequencing reads.](/kontaminant)

[## LotuS2

An ultrafast and highly accurate tool for amplicon sequencing analysis.](/lotus2)

[## Mikado

Combine and filter multiple transcript annotations, providing a tailored and coherent gene annotation.](/mikado)

[## NextClip

Comprehensive quality analysis and read preparation for Nextera long mate pair (LMP) libraries.](/nextclip)

[## NanoOK

A tool for comprehensive analysis of Oxford Nanopore MinION sequence data.](/nanook)

[## Portcullis

Efficient and accurate splice junction prediction from pre-mapped RNAseq reads.](/portcullis)
