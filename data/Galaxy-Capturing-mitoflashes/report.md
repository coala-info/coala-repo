## Description

This workflow is developed for automatic detection and measuring of mitoflashes in time-lapse microscopy images. 

In addition, this workflow can be used for detection and tracking of other spot-like organelles with small motion.

If this workflow helped with the analysis of your data, please do not forget to cite: https://doi.org/10.1097/j.pain.0000000000002642

## Associated Tutorial

This workflows is part of the tutorial [Tracking of mitochondria and capturing mitoflashes](https://training.galaxyproject.org/training-material/topics/imaging/tutorials/detection-of-mitoflashes/tutorial.html), available in the [GTN](https://training.galaxyproject.org)
