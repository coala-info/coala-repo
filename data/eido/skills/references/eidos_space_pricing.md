## Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer refunds?

Eidos Spark is a one-time purchase for a lifetime license. Please note that all sales are final and we do not offer refunds.

   How does Custom Sync work?

After activating your Spark license, you can add your own S3-compatible storage (like AWS S3, Cloudflare R2, or MinIO) to synchronize your data across multiple devices. Giving you full control over your private data. Checkout our [Setup Guide](https://docs.eidos.space/how-to/setup-custom-sync-provider/) for more details.

   Are the free quotas for services (like 100MB Relay) permanent?

During the early stage of eidos.space, we provide free quotas to help users get started. Please note that these free quotas are **not part of the Eidos Spark lifetime license** and may be adjusted or updated in the future as these services evolve. For more information, please refer to the [official documentation](https://docs.eidos.space/services/spark-license/#2-free-quotas-for-add-on-services).
