Research Object Crate for Obitools eDNA metabarcoding

Original URL: https://workflowhub.eu/workflows/655/ro_crate?version=1

Galaxy Workflow created on Galaxy-E european instance, ecology.usegalaxy.eu, related to the Galaxy training tutorial "[Metabarcoding/eDNA through Obitools](https://training.galaxyproject.org/training-material/topics/ecology/tutorials/Obitools-metabarcoding/tutorial.html)" . This workflow allows to analyze DNA metabarcoding / eDNA data produced on Illumina sequencers using the OBITools.
