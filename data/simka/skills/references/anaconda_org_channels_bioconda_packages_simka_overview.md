simka - bioconda | Anaconda.org CMD + K Sign In Sign Up bioconda / simka simka Community Simka and simkaMin are de novo comparative metagenomics tools. Simka represents each dataset as a k-mer spectrum and computes several classical ecological distances between them. SimkaMin outputs approximate (but very similar) results by subsampling the kmer space and requires much less computational resources.
