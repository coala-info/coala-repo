It has several theoretical features that make it particularly suited for model predictive control (MPC) applications. Further numerical modifications have made qpOASES a reliable QP solver, even when tackling semi-definite, ill-posed or degenerated QP problems. Moreover, several interfaces to third-party software like ​Matlab or ​Simulink are provided that make qpOASES easy-to-use even for users without knowledge of C/C++. GETTING STARTED =============== 1.
