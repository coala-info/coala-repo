Please note: This version of FastQTL is no longer maintained. tensorQTL is functionally equivalent and implements multiple additional mapping modes. Running multi-threaded analyses Nominal pass: run_FastQTL_threaded.py ${genotypes} .vcf.gz ${phenotypes} .bed.gz ${prefix} --covariates ${covariates} .txt.gz --window 1e6 --ma_sample_threshold 10 --maf_threshold 0.01 --chunks 100 --threads 10 Permutation pass: run_FastQTL_threaded.py ${genotypes} .vcf.gz ${phenotypes} .bed.gz ${prefix} --covariates ${covariates} .txt.gz --permute 1000 10000 --window 1e6 --ma_sample_threshold 10 --maf_threshold 0.01 --chunks 100 --threads 10 These minor allele filters result in inclusion of genotypes with minor allele frequency ≥ 0.01 and with at least 10 samples containing the minor allele.
