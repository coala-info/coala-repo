# OGC Open Science Persistent Demonstrator (OSPD) - Phase 1

CWL definitions for the Algae Bloom for Water Quality Assessment use case
presented at the [*OGC 129th Member Meeting*][ogc-129th] (2024, Montréal)
and the [*Earth Science Information Partners* (ESIP) 2024 Meeting][ESIP-2024].

Extracted only the relevant components from CRIM, and extend upon them.

[ogc-129th]: https://www.ogc.org/ogc-events/129th-ogc-member-meeting-montreal/
[ESIP-2024]: https://2024julyesipmeeting.sched.com/
