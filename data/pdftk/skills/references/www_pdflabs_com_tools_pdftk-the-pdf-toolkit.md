## PDFtk - The PDF Toolkit

PDFtk is a simple tool for doing everyday things with PDF documents. It comes in three flavors: *PDFtk Free*, *PDFtk Pro*, and our original command-line tool [*PDFtk Server*](/tools/pdftk-server/).

## PDFtk ![Free](free.png)

PDFtk Free is our friendly graphical tool for quickly **merging and splitting PDF documents and pages**. It is free to use for as long as you like.

*Power Users:* PDFtk Free comes with our command-line tool, [PDFtk Server](/tools/pdftk-server/). So you get both the GUI and the command-line interface to PDFtk!

[![PDFtk Free Screenshot](pdftk-free-screenshot-400.png)](/tools/pdftk-the-pdf-toolkit/pdftk-free-screenshot.png)

Available for **Windows 10 and 11**. Download the installer now:

[Download PDFtk Free!](/tools/pdftk-the-pdf-toolkit/pdftk_free-2.02-win-setup.exe)

## PDFtk ![Pro](pro.png)

Use PDFtk Pro to quickly **split, merge, rotate, watermark, stamp and secure PDF pages and documents**. PDFtk Pro is a graphical application that makes working with PDFs as easy as possible.

To see PDFtk Pro in Action, visit our [Guide to PDFtk Pro](/docs/pdftk-pro-guide/).

*Power Users:* PDFtk Pro comes with our command-line tool, [PDFtk Server](/tools/pdftk-server/). So you get both the GUI and the command-line interface to PDFtk!

[![PDFtk Pro Screenshot](pdftk-pro-screenshot-400.png)](/tools/pdftk-the-pdf-toolkit/pdftk-pro-screenshot.png)

Only $3.99!

Available for **Windows 10 and 11**. Your purchase will include a Windows installer download link and a license key. Thank you for your purchase!

[Buy PDFtk Pro!](https://pdflabs.onfastspring.com/pdftk-pro)

## PDFtk ![Server](server.png)

PDFtk Server is our original command-line tool. It is commonly used for server-side processing of PDFs. It is also used by many PC software publishers to give their products the ability to manipulate PDFs.

## About PDFtk

For twenty years, power users have relied on PDFtk for server-side processing of PDF documents. Our command-line PDFtk has been — and always will be — free (as in freedom) software.

Today we also offer a convenient graphical application: [PDFtk Pro](/tools/pdftk-pro/). Your purchase of PDFtk Pro will help fund the continued development of our GPL PDFtk.

Thank you for your support!
