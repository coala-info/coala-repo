SplitsTree is a fairly hefty application for constructing phylogenetic networks of many sorts. Anyone using Neighbor-Net should use the implementation in SplitsTree as it is the fastest and most carefully debugged. For downloads and manual, see the SplitsTree site at www.splitstree.org PopART I worked with Jessica Leigh on PopART, a powerful and user-friendly software package for the construction, display, and analysis of haploytype networks . You can download it from the PhyML website . &nbsp; Phi test The Phi Test is a simple, rapid, and statistically efficient test for recombination. Its performance is comparable with coalescent based methods like LDHat, and yet it can be applied to large alignments with hundreds of sequences. The test, and extensive experimental assessments, can be found in &nbsp; Bruen, T., Phillipe, H. and Bryant, D. 2006. A quick and robust statistical test to detect the presence of recombination.
