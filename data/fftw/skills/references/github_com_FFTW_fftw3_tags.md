It appears that whatever gcc bug caused us to use -march=core-avx2 in 2015 is long gone. I have checked gcc-4.8.5 on centos-7 and oraclelinux-7 (both 64-bit) and on 32-bit debian jessie and in all cases -mavx2 is sufficient. Therefore, given that the original cause for using -march is gone and that using -march causes problems, I am removing the automatic selection of -march=core-avx2 and I am using -mavx2 instead.
