< [MINC](/wiki/MINC "MINC")

Currently MINC (as in file format) and MINC tools are being actively developed by following labs, mostly based in Canada:

* Montreal Neurological Institute: [McConnell Brain Imaging Centre (BIC)](https://www.mcgill.ca/bic/)
  + MRS: Magnetic Resonance Studies Lab
  + [Neuro Imaging and Surgical Technologies lab (NIST)](http://nist.mni.mcgill.ca/)
  + [NOEL: Neuroimaging of Epilepsy Laboratory](http://www.bic.mni.mcgill.ca/~noel/)
* [The McGill Centre for Integrative Neuroscience (MCIN)](http://mcin-cnim.ca/)
* [SIMEXP lab, Research centre of the Montreal Geriatric Institute](http://www.simexp-lab.org/)
* [NeuroRX research](https://www.neurorx.com)
* [Douglas Mental Hospital](http://www.douglas.qc.ca/)
  + [Computational Brain Anatomy Laboratory (CoBrA)](http://cobralab.ca/)
  + [McGill Centre for Studies in Aging](http://aging.mcgill.ca/)
* [Mouse Imaging Centre, Hospital for Sick Children, Toronto Canada](http://www.mouseimaging.ca/)
* [Centre for Advanced Imaging, Brisbane, Australia](http://www.cai.uq.edu.au/)
* [Biospective Inc.](http://www.biospective.com/)
