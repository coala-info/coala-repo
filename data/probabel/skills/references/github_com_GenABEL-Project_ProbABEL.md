It is part of the GenABEL Suite of tools developed by within the GenABEL Project. IMPORTANT NOTICE Early 2018, PolyOmica, the main sponsor of the GenABEL project announced that it would discontinue its support. As PolyOmica was our only sponsor, we regretfully have to announce the likely discontinuation of the GenABEL Project in its current form, unless a new sponsor steps up or someone takes over maintenance and further development of (some of) the tools.
