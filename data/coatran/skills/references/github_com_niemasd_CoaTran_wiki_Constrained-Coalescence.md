# Constrained Coalescence

Suppose that we know that all lineages must coalesce before time ![](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/ef659fc8581204d894014c394880c4ff56824427ccbf628cfbf3c743bbba0f42/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f54) (e.g. if ![](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/ef659fc8581204d894014c394880c4ff56824427ccbf628cfbf3c743bbba0f42/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f54) is the known time of infection of an individual, and we assume transmission is a complete bottleneck).
