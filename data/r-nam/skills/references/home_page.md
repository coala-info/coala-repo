## NAM: Nested Association Mapping

Designed for association studies in nested association mapping (NAM) panels, experimental and random panels. The method is described by Xavier et al. (2015) <[doi:10.1093/bioinformatics/btv448](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btv448)>. It includes tools for genome-wide associations of multiple populations, marker quality control, population genetics analysis, genome-wide prediction, solving mixed models and finding variance components through likelihood and Bayesian methods.
