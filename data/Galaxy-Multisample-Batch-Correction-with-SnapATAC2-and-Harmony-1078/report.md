## Description

This Workflow takes a dataset collection of single-cell ATAC-seq fragments and performs:
- preprocessing
- filtering
- concatenation
- dimension reduction
- batch correction (with Harmony and optionally Scanorama and MNC-correct)
- leiden clustering

* new SnapATAC2 version: from 2.5.3 to 2.6.4
