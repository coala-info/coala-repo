Compute ![](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/ce8f41f6fc5bdb68a2769182387ca11a22742124a3319f6a5bf3f41aed8b02c3/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f78285029) by plugging ![](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/0ac7ddc963c2cdcc359c6df8a513dc42cd1786eedb879e06dcc3fe75e03b59a7/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f50) into the inverse CDF function
