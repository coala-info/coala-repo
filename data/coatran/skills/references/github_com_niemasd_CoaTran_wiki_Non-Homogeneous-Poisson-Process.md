The [non-homogeneous Poisson process](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poisson_point_process#Inhomogeneous_Poisson_point_process) (NHPP) is a generalization of Poisson point process, but instead of having a constant rate ![](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/bf662de56df070d2572246ff9728301dfc26d6ee1a9161cd0186c6b7eba02a7f/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6c616d626461) over time, we have an arbitrary rate function ![](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/c999a9a4cebf507485b1e654eae27f399729b455bce3c705f795098250715576/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6c616d626461287429).
