It reconciles AGP files manually curated in PretextView to rename, reorient, and sort assemblies in preparation for submission. The tool performs three main operations: correcting and splitting AGP files while assigning unlocalized sequences, assigning chromosome names to scaffolds, and reorienting and renaming sequences based on MashMap alignment data.
