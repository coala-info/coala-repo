## bc3net: Gene Regulatory Network Inference with Bc3net

Implementation of the BC3NET algorithm for gene regulatory network inference (de Matos Simoes and Frank Emmert-Streib, Bagging Statistical Network Inference from Large-Scale Gene Expression Data, PLoS ONE 7(3): e33624, <[doi:10.1371/journal.pone.0033624](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0033624)>).
