Currently IRMA is deployed with modules for influenza , ebolavirus and coronavirus . IRMA is free to use and parallelizes computations for both cluster computing and single computer multi-core setups. Please refer to our FAQ below, raise an issue on Github, or contact us for further questions ( do not contact CDC WONDER ). The IRMA manuscript provides more background on the methodology.
