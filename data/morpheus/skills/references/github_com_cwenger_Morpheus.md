In revision 108 and later, proteome databases may be UniProt XML files in addition to FASTA files to support G-PTM searching . When an XML file is loaded, its modifications will be read (masses are read from ptmlist.txt ), added to your variable modifications list, and checked by default. In revision 167 and above, an extra column was added to modifications.tsv to allow for neutral loss modification searching. In revision 255 and above, mzIdentML support necessitated the need to link every modification to UniMod. Therefore, two extra columns, 'UniMod Accession Number' and 'UniMod Name', were were added to modifications.tsv.
