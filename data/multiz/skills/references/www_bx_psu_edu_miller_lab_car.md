Reconstructing Contiguous Regions of an Ancestral Genome Reconstructing Contiguous Regions of an Ancestral Genome This site contains the source code and resultant data from our paper: Ma, J., Zhang, L., Suh, B.B., Raney, B.J., Burhans, R.C., Kent, W.J., Blanchette, M., Haussler, D., and Miller, W. 2006. Reconstructing Contiguous Regions of an Ancestral Genome . Genome Research. Published online before print Sept 18, 2006. We are maintaining two versions of our source code and data. The frozen version, which is static, is a snapshot of our code and data as of paper publication. The latest version, which will change over time, contains our most recent bug fixes and enhancements.
