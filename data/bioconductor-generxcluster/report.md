## geneRxCluster

### Package Description
The package provides some functions for exploring genomic insertion sites originating from two different sources. Possibly, the two sources are two different gene therapy vectors.  Vectors are preferred that target sensitive regions less frequently, motivating the search for localized clusters of insertions and comparison of the clusters formed by integration of different vectors.  Scan statistics allow the discovery of spatial differences in clustering and calculation of False Discovery Rate...
