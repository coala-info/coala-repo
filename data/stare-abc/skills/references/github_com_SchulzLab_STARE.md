If this sounds interesting to you, here's a full documentation on Read the Docs . References STARE and its application are described in our publication: Hecker, D., Behjati Ardakani, F., Karollus, A., Gagneur, J., &amp; Schulz, M. H. (2023). The adapted Activity-By-Contact model for enhancer–gene assignment and its application to single-cell data. Bioinformatics, 39(2), btad062. https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btad062 The results of our analyses are available via Zenodo .
