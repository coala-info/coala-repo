## structSSI: Multiple Testing for Hypotheses with Hierarchical or Group Structure

Performs multiple testing corrections that take specific structure
of hypotheses into account, as described in Sankaran & Holmes (2014)
<[doi:10.18637/jss.v059.i13](https://doi.org/10.18637/jss.v059.i13)>.
