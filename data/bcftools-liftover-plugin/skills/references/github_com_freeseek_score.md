We encourage users to adopt the GWAS-VCF specification rather than the GWAS-SSF specification promoted by the GWAS catalog as the latter is affected by issues and furthermore we believe that many common uses are better addressed by using the more general VCF specification. If you are planning to publish your summary statistics, we encourage you to submit them as GWAS-VCF files or as both GWAS-VCF and as GWAS-SSF files.
