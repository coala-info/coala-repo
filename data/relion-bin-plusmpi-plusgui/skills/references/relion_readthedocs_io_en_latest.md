# RELION[¶](#relion "Link to this heading")

relion (for **RE**gularised **LI**kelihood **O**ptimisatio**N**, pronounce rely-on) is a software package that employs an empirical Bayesian approach for electron cryo-microscopy (cryo-EM) structure determination.
It is developed in the group of [Sjors Scheres](https://www2.mrc-lmb.cam.ac.uk/groups/scheres/) at the [MRC Laboratory of Molecular Biology](https://www2.mrc-lmb.cam.ac.uk).

Note

As the [cryo-EM resolution revolution](https://science.sciencemag.org/content/343/6178/1443.summary) progresses, software protectionism is also on the rise.
We are proud to distribute relion under a *GPLv2* license.
This means that anyone (including commercial users) can download, use and modify relion for free.
If relion is useful in your work, please do make sure that our [Team](Team.html) members are credited appropriately and cite [our papers](https://www2.mrc-lmb.cam.ac.uk/groups/scheres/publications.html).
