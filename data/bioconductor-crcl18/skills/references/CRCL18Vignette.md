Introduction

The package contain data objects of miRNA and mRNA profile of 18 colorectal
cancer cell line classfied according to CRC-Assigner [?]. The ExpressionSet
include samples information (phenoData) and probe (genes) information. Data
were respectivelly for mRNA and miRNA with Illumina BeadChip and Illumina
smallTruSeq method.

> library(CRCL18)
