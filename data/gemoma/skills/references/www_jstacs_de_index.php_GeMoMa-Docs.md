The tool is multi-threaded and can utilize all compute cores on one machine, but not distributed as for instance in a compute cluster. It basically runs: Extract RNA-seq evidence (ERE) , DenoiseIntrons , Extractor , external search (tblastn or mmseqs), Gene Model Mapper (GeMoMa) , GeMoMa Annotation Filter (GAF) , and AnnnotationFinalizer .
