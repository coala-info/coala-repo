# changepoint: Methods for Changepoint Detection

Implements various mainstream and specialised changepoint methods for finding single and multiple changepoints within data.  Many popular non-parametric and frequentist methods are included.  The cpt.mean(), cpt.var(), cpt.meanvar() functions should be your first point of call.</p>
