It has demonstrated excellent performance across a wide range of datasets and applications. MSFragger is suitable for standard shotgun proteomics analyses as well as large datasets (including timsTOF PASEF data), enzyme unconstrained searches (e.g., peptidome), open database searches (e.g., precursor mass tolerance set to hundreds of Daltons) for identification of modified peptides, and glycopeptide identification (N-linked and O-linked). MSFragger is available freely for academic research and educational purposes only, in accordance with the terms at https://msfragger.arsci.com/upgrader/MSFragger-LICENSE.pdf.
