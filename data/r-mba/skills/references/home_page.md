## MBA: Multilevel B-Spline Approximation

Functions to interpolate irregularly and regularly spaced data using Multilevel B-spline Approximation (MBA). Functions call portions of the SINTEF Multilevel B-spline Library written by Øyvind Hjelle which implements methods developed by Lee, Wolberg and Shin (1997; <[doi:10.1109/2945.620490](https://doi.org/10.1109/2945.620490)>).
