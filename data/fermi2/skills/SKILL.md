## Overview
The fermi2 toolset is designed for advanced bioinformatics tasks, specifically focusing on the exploration and manipulation of FMD-indexes. It is a component of the fermi suite, which is used for de novo genome assembly. fermi2 itself is not a full assembler but provides core functionalities for graph-based sequence analysis, particularly when working with FMD-indexes constructed by tools like `fermi` or `ropebwt2`. It's useful for tasks that involve understanding the structure of assembled genomes or analyzing complex sequence relationships.

## Usage Instructions

fermi2 is a command-line tool. Its primary function is to explore FMD-indexes as graphs. It relies on pre-constructed indexes.
