## What is metaGEM?

`metaGEM` is a Snakemake-based workflow designed to generate high quality metagenome assembled genomes from short read paired-end data, reconstruct genome scale metabolic models, and perform community metabolic interaction simulations on high performance computing clusters. In the [metaGEM paper](https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkab815) we apply the workflow to lab culture, human gut, plant associated, bulk soil, and TARA oceans metagenomic samples.

## Quickstart

The fastest way for you to start submitting jobs with `metaGEM` is to follow this updated [setup guide](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/tree/master/config). Alternatively, you may have a look at the older [quickstart guide](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Quickstart) or the [manual setup guide](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Manual-setup).

## Tutorial

A detailed tutorial of the `metaGEM` core workflow is demonstrated [here](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/unseenbio_metaGEM).

## Frequently asked questions

You can find answers to FAQs [here](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/FAQs).

## Workflow

The workflow below is adapted from Figure 1 of the `metaGEM` manuscript:

![metawrapfigs_v2 002](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/35606471/103545679-ceb71580-4e99-11eb-9862-084115121980.jpeg)

In the following pages you can find documentation regarding the implementation of each tool within the Snakefile. For a more hands on demonstration of *how* to use `metaGEM` please see the tutorial above.

### Core

1. [Quality filter reads with fastp](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Quality-filter-reads-with-fastp)
2. [Assembly with megahit](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Assembly-with-megahit)
3. [Draft bin sets with CONCOCT, MaxBin2, and MetaBAT2](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Draft-bin-sets-with-CONCOCT%2CMaxBin2%2C-and-MetaBAT2)
4. [Refine & reassemble bins with metaWRAP](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Refine-%26-reassemble-bins-with-metaWRAP)
5. [Taxonomic assignment with GTDB-tk](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Taxonomic-assignment-with-GTDBtk)
6. [Relative abundances with bwa](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Relative-abundances-with-bwa-and-samtools)
7. [Reconstruct & evaluate genome-scale metabolic models with CarveMe and memote](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Reconstruct-%26-evaluate-genome-scale-metabolic-models-with-CarveMe-and-memote)
8. [Species metabolic coupling analysis with SMETANA](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Species-metabolic-coupling-analysis-with-SMETANA)

### Bonus

9. [Growth rate estimation](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Growth-rate-estimation)
10. [Pangenome analysis based on MAGs](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Pangenome-analysis-based-on-MAGs)
11. [Eukaryotic draft bins with EukRep and EukCC](https://github.com/franciscozorrilla/metaGEM/wiki/Eukaryotic-draft-bins-with-EukRep-and-EukCC)
