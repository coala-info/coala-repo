AffyBatch instance ecoliLeucine

Description

The Leucine-responsive regulatory protein (Lrp) is thought to regulate the expression of multiple
genes and operons in bacteria. Two strains lrp+ and lrp- were studied to investigate which genes
are subject to changes in expression levels.

The raw data were taken from CyberT website. The data for 8 chips (4 lrp+ and 4 lrp-).
