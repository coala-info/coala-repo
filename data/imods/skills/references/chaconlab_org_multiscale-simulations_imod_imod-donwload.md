# iMOD Archive

Here you can get the latest iMOD distributions in your favorite flavor. The current stable version is 1.04. We also include the docking examples used in the tutorials and a benchmark of 46 proteins and 22 RNAs structure pairs in a open to closed transitions used for validating purposes.  By clicking the Download button, you acknowledge that you have read and agree our [Software Licensing Agreement](/downloads/License.txt).
