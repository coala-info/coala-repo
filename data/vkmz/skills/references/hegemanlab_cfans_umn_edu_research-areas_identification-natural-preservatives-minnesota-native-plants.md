# Identification of Natural Preservatives from Minnesota Native Plants

## Collaborators:

**[Don Wyse](http://agronomy.cfans.umn.edu/department-directory/donald-l-wyse),**Department of Agronomy and Plant Genetics

[**Aveda Corp.**](http://www.aveda.com/discover/index.tmpl) (a subsidiary of Esteé Lauder)

The goal of this project is to identify new sources of targeted activities for cosmetic formulations (including: antimicrobial, antioxidant and others to be decided) from North American native plant species starting with species native to Minnesota.  Minnesota native plants should provide a sustainable local alternative to other formulation ingredients that can be produced within Aveda’s green and organic ingredient sourcing policy.

![Fireweed plant.](/sites/hegemanlab.cfans.umn.edu/files/files/media/fireweed.jpg)

Fireweed (*Epilobium angustifolium*) is one of several Minnesota native or naturalized plant species *being investigated as a source of natural perservatives.*
