# erah: Automated Spectral Deconvolution, Alignment, and Metabolite
Identification in GC/MS-Based Untargeted Metabolomics

Automated compound deconvolution, alignment across samples, and identification of metabolites by spectral library matching in Gas Chromatography - Mass spectrometry (GC-MS) untargeted metabolomics. Outputs a table with compound names, matching scores and the integrated area of the compound for each sample. Package implementation is described in Domingo-Almenara et al.
