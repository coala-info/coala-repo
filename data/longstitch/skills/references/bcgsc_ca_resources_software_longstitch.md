LongStitch runs in up to three stages, which includes initial assembly correction (Tigmint-long), followed by two incremental scaffolding stages (ntLink and ARKS-long). If you use LongStitch in your research, please cite: Coombe L, Li JX, Lo T, Wong J, Nikolic V, Warren RL and Birol I. LongStitch: high-quality genome assembly correction and scaffolding using long reads.
