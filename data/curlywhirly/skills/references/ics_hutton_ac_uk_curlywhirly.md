It comfortably handles large-scale genetic diversity data with plots containing tens of thousands of data points on relatively modest hardware. To explore datasets in detail, a comprehensive hierarchical categorization and filtering system allows for fine-grained filtering and selection of data points. Visit the download page to get the latest version of CurlyWhirly 1.21.08.16 (16th August 2021). CurlyWhirly features: 3D display of multi-dimensional data. Rotate the graph by dragging on the display. Zoom in and out of the data set. Automatic rotation. Filtering of coloured data points by selecting/de-selecting categories.
