It is primarily aimed at drawing consensus diagrams, but can also draw single RNA molecules. R2R is freely available under the GNU Public License, and runs as a command-line program under UNIX-like systems such as Linux, MacOS Darwin or Cygwin on Windows. R2R was written by Zasha Weinberg.
