The general idea is to include only established tech companies for which a significant portion of the workforce works remotely. Established means that each company is either well-funded or profitable with at least 50 employees, and tech means that they have more than a handful of software development roles. To make this list more useful to a global audience, companies that limit their hiring of engineers to a small set of rich countries are no longer included. Pull requests are welcome. Please keep the list in alphabetical order. Background: I started this list back in 2015 as a way of finding companies I may want to work with. This turned out well, as I started working remotely back in 2017. With remote work becoming mainstream since 2020, I've narrowed down the list to focus on companies that hire globally. You can visit my website to read some of my 2018 reflections on remote data science . You can also check out this video of a 2019 talk I gave on a day in the life of a remote data scientist . These days, I still work remotely, but as an independent AI/ML success architect . TL;DR: Remote work is awesome! :) ⚡ Running a climate tech venture and looking for help shipping AI/ML solutions?
