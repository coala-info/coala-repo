## iC10: A Copy Number and Expression-Based Classifier for Breast Tumours

Implementation of the classifier described in the paper Ali HR et al (2014) <[doi:10.1186/s13059-014-0431-1](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-014-0431-1)>. It uses copy number and/or expression form breast cancer data, trains a Tibshirani's 'pamr' classifier with the features available and predicts the iC10 group.
