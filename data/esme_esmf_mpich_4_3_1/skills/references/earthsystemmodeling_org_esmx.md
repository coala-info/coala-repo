The goals of ESMX are to: simplify standing up new NUOPC-based systems, promote hierarchical model component testing, reduce the cost of maintaining NUOPC-based modeling systems, improve alignment and interoperability between different NUOPC-based systems, and provide a fast and coordinated roll-out strategy for new ESMF/NUOPC features. Download and Install ESMX is included with the ESMF distribution . No separate download or installation is required. Documentation See the README.md for the current ESMX documentation.
