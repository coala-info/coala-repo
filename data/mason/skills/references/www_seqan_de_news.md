Dear SeqAn users, supporters, and subscribers,

08

Sharg 1.1.0 released

Dear SeqAn users, supporters, and subscribers,

We have just released a new Version of our C++ command line parsing lib: Sharg 1.1.0
Check it out on GitHub: https://github.com/seqan/sharg-parser

It includes a few major bug fixes and Gitpod and CWL support! Best update your apps now.

[Read more](/news/2023-05-08-sharg-release.html)

October, 2022

10

Sharg 1.0.0 released

Dear SeqAn users, supporters, and subscribers,

We are thrilled to announce the first **Sharg parser release: 1.0.0** with most of the API being **stable**.

We have outsourced the SeqAn3 Argument Parser to its own light-weight, dependency-free repository:
The [**Sharg parser**](https://github.com/seqan/sharg-parser).

[Read more](/news/2022-10-10-sharg-release.html)
