[PhyloAln v1.2.0](/huangyh45/PhyloAln/releases/tag/v1.2.0)

1.reorganize and optimize the parallel and storage operations, reduce the memory use and speed up 18 - 229 % (spend 30 - 85 % of the time)
2.add MMseqs2 and Infernal (experimental) as the optimal search tool
3.add the step to polish (trim or extend) the start and end terminals of the hits
4.use a global assembly method to replace the local (greedy) method
5.optimize the outgroup score calculation of foreign decontamination to avoid the impact of missing regions in the defined outgroup sequences
6.add the options to independently control the symbol representing unknown translated amino acids (--unknow\_prot), to search only the first position of the codon sequences (--codon), and to try to connect the target fragments split by introns in the genomic sequences (similar with gene prediction, --intron\_len and the easy modes -e gene\_xxx2genome based on this option)
