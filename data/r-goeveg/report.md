# goeveg: Functions for Community Data and Ordinations

A collection of functions useful in (vegetation) community analyses and ordinations. Includes automatic species selection for ordination diagrams, NMDS stress/scree plots, species response curves, merging of taxa as well as calculation and sorting of synoptic tables.</p>
