Statistics-Descriptive CPAN Record http://metacpan.org/release/Statistics-Descriptive Description Statistics-Descriptive is a module to perform Statistical Analysis on numeric data. It can retrieve such things as the mean (or average), the median, the standard deviation, a histogram / frequency analysis, etc. Statistics-Descriptive has seen work from several people, the latest of whom was Colin Kuskie , and was recently adopted by us.
