## isva: Independent Surrogate Variable Analysis

Independent Surrogate Variable Analysis is an algorithm
for feature selection in the presence of potential confounding
factors (see Teschendorff AE et al 2011, <[doi:10.1093/bioinformatics/btr171](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btr171)>).
