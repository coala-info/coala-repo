## gMWT: Generalized Mann-Whitney Type Tests

Generalized Mann-Whitney type tests based on probabilistic
indices and new diagnostic plots, for the underlying manuscript see Fischer, Oja (2015) <[doi:10.18637/jss.v065.i09](https://doi.org/10.18637/jss.v065.i09)>.
