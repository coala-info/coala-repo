It is based on two prominent medical dictionary projects: OpenMedSpel by e-MedTools MTH-Med-Spel-Chek by Rajasekharan N. of MT-Herald The two sources have been merged, deduplicated, corrected, and converted into a Hunspell dictionary file. Details Number of Terms: 90142 Contents: drug names (up-to-date with FDA-approvals as of 2014-04-02, trade and generic names), anatomical terms, dermatological terms, internal medicine terms, surgical terms, DSM-IV terms, ICD-9 terms, and many more Author: (c) 2014-2017 Aristotelis P.
