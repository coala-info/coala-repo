MAXIT can: Read and write PDB and mmCIF format files, and translate between file formats. Perform consistency checks on coordinates, sequence, and crystal data. Automatically construct, transform, and merge information between formats Align residue numbering in the coordinates with the sequence Reorder and rename atoms in standard and nonstandard residues and ligands according to the Chemical Component Dictionary Assign ligands the same chain IDs as the adjacent polymers Detect missing or additional atoms MAXIT runs on the following platforms: CentOS 7, Alma 8, and Ubuntu Installation and usage notes for the source distribution of Maxit Suite Click here to download source distribution of Maxit Suite. &nbsp; &nbsp; &copy; RCSB PDB &nbsp;&nbsp;
