PhyloPhlAn 3.0 is an accurate, rapid, and easy-to-use method for large-scale microbial genome characterization and phylogenetic analysis at multiple levels of resolution. PhyloPhlAn 3.0 can assign both genomes and metagenome-assembled genomes (MAGs) to species-level genome bins (SGBs). PhyloPhlAn 3.0 can reconstruct strain-level phylogenies using clade-specific maximally informative phylogenetic markers, and can also scale to very-large phylogenies comprising &gt;17,000 microbial species.
