## Use of Information

You may visit this website without providing any personal information. When accessing the site, certain technical data such as your IP address, browser type, date and time of access, and the last page visited are automatically recorded in server log files. This information is collected to ensure the security and stability of our services, to analyse usage in an aggregated and anonymised form, and to continuously improve the website. These data do not allow direct identification of individuals and are not combined with other sources.

We use **Google Analytics** in a **cookieless configuration**. In this mode, no cookies or other tracking technologies are stored on your device. No third-party cookies are used on this website.

## Data Storage and Retention

For the operation of the database search service, certain data must be stored temporarily. When a user submits a query, the text of the query is stored in plain text, and the associated e-mail address is stored in encrypted form. This information is retained solely to ensure the functionality of the service and to allow users to manage or delete their own query records. The data are never used for profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

All stored information is automatically deleted after a maximum period of **six months**, or sooner if the user actively deletes their record via the user interface, whichever occurs first. When a record expires or is deleted, the corresponding query string and e-mail address are removed from our systems. If the same e-mail address is associated with other query records still within their retention period, those will be retained until their respective deletion conditions are met.

In addition, during an active browser session, certain non-personal settings — such as whether a pop-up should reappear or which databases have been selected — may be stored locally for convenience. These temporary settings are kept only while the search session remains open and are automatically removed once the browser window is closed or the session expires. They are not shared with third parties and do not involve the use of cookies.

All data are processed and stored on secure systems under the responsibility of ETH Zurich. Appropriate technical and organisational measures are in place to protect data from unauthorised access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. Once a record has been deleted, it cannot be recovered.
