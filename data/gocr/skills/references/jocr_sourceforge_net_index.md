GOCR open-source character recognition Index | Download | Screenshots | Examples | Developers | Support | Links About GOCR is an OCR (Optical Character Recognition) program, developed under the GNU Public License. It converts scanned images of text back to text files. Joerg Schulenburg started the program, and was leading the team of developers on SF, and after 2010 still manages the package at a (very) low time base. GOCR can be used with different front-ends, which makes it very easy to port to different OSes and architectures. It can open many different image formats, and its quality have been improving in a daily basis until 2010. GOCR or JOCR? The original name is GOCR. It's what is used internally in the sources. But, when registering the site at Sourceforge, gocr was already taken. So, it's kind of both as long it will be hosted at SF.
