Magphi is a tool built to help in extracting stretches of genetic sequence and possible annotations from a genome placed within an area of interest defined by two seed sequences. Magphi can work with GFF3 and Fasta files. In this wiki you can find a deeper dive into how Magphi works and how it may help you in your research. On the right of this screen you can find different tabs to read. We recommend you start with 'Quick start' or 'How it works' to get you going.
