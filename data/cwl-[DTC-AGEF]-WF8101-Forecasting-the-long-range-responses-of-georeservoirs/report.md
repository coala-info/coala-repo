## Description

The advanced earthquake catalog is built from existing applications on the EPISODES platform (step 1,3, 4 in Figure 4.2) and a newly developed application for phase association (step 2) and an application for catalog homogenization (step 5). The last application is designed to facilitate the creation of a homogenized earthquake catalog, ensuring consistency across key parameters such as location (X, Y, Z), time (T), and magnitude (M). This is achieved by standardizing various magnitude measurements, including moment magnitude, local magnitude, duration magnitude, among others, to maintain uniform features throughout the catalog. Utilizing the Maximum Curvature Method (Step 5), an existing application of the EPISODES Platform, we calculated Mc (magnitude of completeness) for the comprehensive catalog. The advanced catalog serves as the foundation for our subsequent analyses of spatial and temporal density.
