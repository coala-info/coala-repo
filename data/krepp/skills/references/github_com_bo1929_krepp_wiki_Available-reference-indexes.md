# Available reference indexes

[Jump to bottom](#wiki-pages-box)

Ali Osman Berk Şapcı edited this page Mar 13, 2026
·
[8 revisions](/bo1929/krepp/wiki/Available-reference-indexes/_history)

Instead of building an index from scratch, you may use one of the public ones. We primarily offer microbial indices, consisting of bacterial and archaeal genomes. We plan to publish eukaryotic indices in the future.

Some of these indices overlap, and you could just pick the one that you can afford in terms of memory available on your machine.
Simply copy the link, and use `wget` or `curl`. You may need to use `wget --no-check-certificate` or `curl -k`.

The indexes listed here are tar archies and some are gzip compressed; you can extract the files via `tar -xvf` or `tar -xzvf`, respectively, before using krepp with them.

## Prokaryotic indices

[comment]: # (GTDB release 220 (all archaeal and bacterial genomes, **201 GB**) - [index\*](https://kreppref.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/index_prok-GTDB_R09_RS220-k29w43-h13.tar), [tree](https://data.ace.uq.edu.au/public/gtdb/data/releases/release220/220.0/), [metadata](https://data.ace.uq.edu.au/public/gtdb/data/releases/release220/220.0/))

* Web of Life - v2 (15,493 archaeal and bacterial genomes, **41 GB**): [index](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/index_WoLv2-k29w35-h14.tar.gz), [tree](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/backbone_tree-WoLv2.nwk.gz), [metadata](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/metadata-WoLv2.tsv.gz)
* Web of Life - v1 (10,576 archaeal and bacterial genomes, **67 GB**): [index](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/index_WoLv1-k29w35-h14.tar.gz), [tree](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/backbone_tree-WoLv1.nwk.gz), [metadata](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/metadata-WoLv1.tsv.gz)
* RefSeq snapshot - medium (50,752 archaeal and bacterial genomes, **137 GB**): [index](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/index_CAMI2dedup-k30w37-h14.tar.gz), [tree](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/backbone_tree-CAMI2_dedup-pruned.nwk.gz), [taxonomy](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/ncbi_taxonomy-CAMI2.tar.gz)
* RefSeq snapshot - large (123,853 archaeal and bacterial genomes, **184 GB**): [index](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/index_CAMI2dup-k30w37-h14.tar.gz), [tree](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/backbone_tree-CAMI2_dup-pruned.nwk.gz), [taxonomy](https://ter-trees.ucsd.edu/data/krepp/misc/ncbi_taxonomy-CAMI2.tar.gz)

\*: The backbone tree comprises bac120 (bacterial) and ar53 (archaeal) phylogenies (merged at the root).

The reported genome IDs themselves may not be informative, especially for WoL.
You can use the provided metadata and taxonomy files to analyze your distance estimates further, perhaps for taxonomic abundance profiling. Similarly, the backbone trees could be used for UniFrac computation.
