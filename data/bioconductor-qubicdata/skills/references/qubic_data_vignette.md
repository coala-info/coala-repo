Introduction

The package contain gene expression data matrix for experiments with E.coli from Many Microbe Microarrays
Database (M3D)(Faith et al. 2008).

Usage

library(QUBICdata)
data("ecoli")
data("ecoli.weight")

References

Faith, Jeremiah J, Michael E Driscoll, Vincent A Fusaro, Elissa J Cosgrove, Boris Hayete, Frank S Juhn,
Stephen J Schneider, and Timothy S Gardner. 2008. “Many Microbe Microarrays Database: Uniformly
Normalized Affymetrix Compendia with Structured Experimental Metadata.” Nucleic Acids Research 36
(suppl 1): D866–D870. https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkm815.
