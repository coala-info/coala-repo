Sequence assembler programs (such as Velvet , SPAdes , Trinity and MEGAHIT ) carry out assembly by building a graph, from which contigs are generated. By granting easy access to these assembly graphs, Bandage allows users to better understand, troubleshoot and improve their assemblies. With Bandage, you can zoom and pan around the graph, customise the visualisation, search for sequences, extract sequences, and more.
