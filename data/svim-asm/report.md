## svim-asm

### Tool Description
SVIM-asm is a structural variant caller for haploid and diploid genome assemblies. It allows to detect five different types of structural variants (deletions, insertions, tandem duplications, interspersed duplications, and inversions) from assembly-to-assembly alignments.
