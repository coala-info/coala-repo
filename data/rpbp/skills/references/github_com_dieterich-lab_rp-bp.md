Isolation and deep-sequencing of ribosome-protected RNA fragments (ribosome footprints) provides a genome-wide snapshot of the translatome at sub-codon resolution. Rp-Bp is an unsupervised Bayesian approach to predict translated open reading frames (ORFs) from ribosome profiles. Rp-Bp can be used for ORF discovery, or simply to estimate periodicity in a set of Ribo-seq samples. When used for ORF discovery, Rp-Bp automatically classifies ORFs into different biotypes or categories, relative to their host transcript. Rp-Bp comes with two interactive dashboards or web applications , one for read and periodicity quality control, the other to facilitate Ribo-seq ORFs discovery.
