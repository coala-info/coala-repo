Anything accepted by BioPython is fine [fasta] --codonPoses {1,2,3,12,13,23} Codon position(s) to be extracted [12] --outAln &lt;file&gt; output file name Author Guanliang MENG Citation Currently I have no plan to publish extract-codon-alignment . However, if you use this program in your analysis, or you "steal" the idea/codes of this program into your script, I should be one of the co-authors in your publication!!!
