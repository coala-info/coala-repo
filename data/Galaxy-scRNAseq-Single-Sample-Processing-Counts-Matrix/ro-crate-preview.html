Research Object Crate for scRNAseq Single Sample Processing Counts Matrix

Original URL: https://workflowhub.eu/workflows/514/ro_crate?version=3

Take a scRNAseq counts matrix from a single sample, and perform basic QC with scanpy. Then, do further processing by making a UMAP and clustering. Produces a processed AnnData object. Depreciated: use individual workflows insead for multiple samples
