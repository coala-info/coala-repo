The workflows in this collection are from the '16S Microbial Analysis with mothur' tutorial for analysis of 16S data (Saskia Hiltemann, Bérénice Batut, Dave Clements), adapted for piepline use on galaxy australia (Ahmed Mehdi). The workflows developed in galaxy use mothur software package developed by Schloss et al https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/19801464/.
