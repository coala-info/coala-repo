LoRMA LoRMA LoRMA has moved. The newest versions are available here . LoRMA is a tool for correcting sequencing errors in long reads such those produced by Pacific Biosciences sequencing machines. Publication: L. Salmela, R. Walve, E. Rivals, and E. Ukkonen: Accurate selfcorrection of errors in long reads using de Bruijn graphs. Accepted to Bioinformatics (Also in RECOMB-Seq 2016).
