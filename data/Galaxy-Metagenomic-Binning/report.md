## Description

Binning workflows that uses abundance information and performs binning of metagenomic contigs using 4 different binners as well as bin refinement.

## Associated Tutorial

This workflows is part of the tutorial [Binning of metagenomic sequencing data](https://training.galaxyproject.org/training-material/topics/microbiome/tutorials/metagenomics-binning/tutorial.html), available in the [GTN](https://training.galaxyproject.org)
