It supports three alignment modes: local (Smith-Waterman), global (Needleman-Wunsch) and semi-global alignment (overlap), and three gap modes: linear, affine and convex (piecewise affine). It also supports Intel SSE4.1+ and AVX2 vectorization (marginally faster due to high latency shifts), SIMDe and dispatching.
