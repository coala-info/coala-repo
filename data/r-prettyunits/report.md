# prettyunits: Pretty, Human Readable Formatting of Quantities

Pretty, human readable formatting of quantities.
    Time intervals: '1337000' -&gt; '15d 11h 23m 20s'.
    Vague time intervals: '2674000' -&gt; 'about a month ago'.
    Bytes: '1337' -&gt; '1.34 kB'.
    Rounding: '99' with 3 significant digits -&gt; '99.0'
    p-values: '0.00001' -&gt; '&lt;0.0001'.
    Colors: '#FF0000' -&gt; 'red'.
    Quantities: '1239437' -&gt; '1.24 M'.</p>
