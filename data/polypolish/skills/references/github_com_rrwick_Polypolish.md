Unlike other polishers, Polypolish uses SAM files where each read has been aligned to all possible locations (not just a single best location). This allows it to repair errors in repeat regions that other alignment-based polishers cannot fix. Polypolish is also a conservative polisher, so it is very unlikely to introduce new errors during polishing. For installation instructions, usage, deeper explanations and more, head over to the Polypolish wiki ! This is the original paper describing Polypolish: Wick RR, Holt KE. Polypolish: short-read polishing of long-read bacterial genome assemblies. PLOS Computational Biology. 2022. doi:10.1371/journal.pcbi.1009802. And this is a follow-up paper that describes Polypolish v0.6.0: Bouras G, Judd LM, Edwards RA, Vreugde S, Stinear TP, Wick RR. How low can you go? Short-read polishing of Oxford Nanopore bacterial genome assemblies. Microbial Genomics. 2024. doi:10.1099/mgen.0.001254.
