## AHPathbankDbs

### Package Description
The package provides a comprehensive mapping table of metabolites and proteins linked to PathBank pathways. The tables include HMDB, KEGG, ChEBI, CAS, Drugbank, Uniprot IDs. The tables are provided for each of the 10 species ("Homo sapiens", "Escherichia coli", "Mus musculus", "Arabidopsis thaliana", "Saccharomyces cerevisiae", "Bos taurus", "Caenorhabditis elegans", "Rattus norvegicus", "Drosophila melanogaster", and "Pseudomonas aeruginosa").
