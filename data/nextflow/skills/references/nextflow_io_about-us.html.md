## About Nextflow

Nextflow is a free and open-source software distributed under the Apache 2.0 licence, developed by
[Seqera.](http://www.seqera.io)
The software is used by scientists and engineers to write, deploy and share data-intensive, highly scalable,
workflows on any infrastructure.

Check it out the
[project repository](https://github.com/nextflow-io/nextflow)
on GitHub for details.
