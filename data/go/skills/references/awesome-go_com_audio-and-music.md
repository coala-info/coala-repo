# Audio and Music

Libraries for manipulating audio and music.

* [beep - A simple library for playback and audio manipulation.](/audio-and-music/beep-gopxl-github/)
* [flac - Native Go FLAC encoder/decoder with support for FLAC streams.](/audio-and-music/flac-mewkiz-github/)
* [gaad - Native Go AAC bitstream parser.](/audio-and-music/gaad-comcast-github/)
* [go-mpris - Client for mpris dbus interfaces.](/audio-and-music/go-mpris-leberkleber-github/)
* [GoAudio - Native Go Audio Processing Library.](/audio-and-music/goaudio-dylanmeeus-github/)
* [gosamplerate - libsamplerate bindings for go.](/audio-and-music/gosamplerate-dh1tw-github/)
* [id3v2 - ID3 decoding and encoding library for Go.](/audio-and-music/id3v2-bogem-github/)
* [malgo - Mini audio library.](/audio-and-music/malgo-gen2brain-github/)
* [minimp3 - Lightweight MP3 decoder library.](/audio-and-music/minimp3-tosone-github/)
* [music-theory - Music theory models in Go.](/audio-and-music/music-theory-go-music-theory-github/)
* [Oto - A low-level library to play sound on multiple platforms.](/audio-and-music/oto-hajimehoshi-github/)
* [PortAudio - Go bindings for the PortAudio audio I/O library.](/audio-and-music/portaudio-gordonklaus-github/)
