By leveraging the power of Snakemake , these workflows promote: Reproducibility Snakemake workflows produce consistent results, making it easier to share and validate scientific findings. Scalability Snakemake workflows can be executed on various environments, from local machines to clusters and clouds. Modularity Snakemake workflows allow easy customization and extension, enabling users to adapt them to their needs.
