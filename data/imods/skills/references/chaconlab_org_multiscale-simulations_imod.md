iMOD is an versatile toolkit to perform Normal Mode Analysis (NMA) in internal coordinates (IC) on both protein and nucleic acid atomic structures. Vibrational analysis, motion animations, morphing trajectories and Monte-Carlo simulations can be easily carried out at different scales of resolution using this toolkit. You can also have access to the latest version from our [iMODS](http://imods.chaconlab.org/) online server
