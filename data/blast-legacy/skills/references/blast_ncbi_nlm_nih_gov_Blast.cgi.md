# Basic Local Alignment Search Tool

BLAST finds regions of similarity between biological sequences. The program compares nucleotide or
protein sequences to sequence databases and calculates the statistical
significance.
Learn more

The Basic Local Alignment Search Tool (BLAST) finds regions of local
similarity between sequences. The program compares nucleotide or
protein sequences to sequence databases and calculates the statistical
significance of matches. BLAST can be used to infer functional and
evolutionary relationships between sequences as well as help identify
members of gene families.

## Specialized searches

* [SmartBLAST

  Find proteins highly similar to your query](https://blast.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/smartblast/?LINK_LOC=BlastHomeLink "SmartBlast")
* [Primer-BLAST

  Design primers specific to your PCR template](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/tools/primer-blast/index.cgi?LINK_LOC=BlastHome "Find primers")
* [Global Align

  Compare two sequences across their entire span (Needleman-Wunsch)](Blast.cgi?PAGE_TYPE=BlastSearch&PROG_DEF=blastn&BLAST_PROG_DEF=blastn&BLAST_SPEC=GlobalAln&LINK_LOC=BlastHomeLink "Global alignment")
* [CD-search

  Find conserved domains in your sequence](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Structure/cdd/wrpsb.cgi "Search conserved domains on a protein")
* [IgBLAST

  Search immunoglobulins and T cell receptor sequences](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/igblast/ "Search immunoglobulins")
* [VecScreen

  Search sequences for vector contamination](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/tools/vecscreen/ "Screen a Sequence Using VecScreen")
* [CDART

  Find sequences with similar conserved domain architecture](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Structure/lexington/lexington.cgi?cmd=rps "Find a protein sequence's domain architecture")
* [Multiple Alignment

  Align sequences using domain and protein constraints](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/tools/cobalt/cobalt.cgi?LINK_LOC=BlastHomeLink "Compute a multiple protein sequence alignment")
