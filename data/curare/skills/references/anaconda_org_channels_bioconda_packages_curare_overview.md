Define standardized pipelines customized for your specific workflow without the necessity of installing all the tools by yourself. Curare is implemented in Python and uses the power of Snakemake and Conda to build and execute the defined workflows. Its modulized structure and the simplicity of Snakemake enables developers to create new and advanced workflow steps.
