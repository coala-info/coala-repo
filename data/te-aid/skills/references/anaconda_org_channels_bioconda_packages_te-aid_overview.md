It inputs a TE consensus sequence (fasta format) and requires a reference genome (in fasta as well). TE-Aid produces 4 figures reporting i) the genomic hits with divergence to consensus, ii) the genomic coverage of the consensus, iii) a self dot-plot, and iv) a structure analysis including TIR and LTR suggestions, open reading frames (ORFs) and TE protein hit annotation.
