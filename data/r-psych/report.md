# psych: Procedures for Psychological, Psychometric, and Personality
Research

A general purpose toolbox developed originally for personality, psychometric theory and experimental psychology.  Functions are primarily for multivariate analysis and scale construction using factor analysis, principal component analysis, cluster analysis and reliability analysis, although others provide basic descriptive statistics.
