Help DANPOS (5) Profile Profile is a function in DANPOS for analyzing the distribution of a chromatin feature flanking each given group of genomic sites or regions, such as transcription start sites, gene bodies, or enhancers. The distribution can be presented in either heat map or average density plot. See examples below: Example 1.
