| ProMod3[¶](#promod3 "Link to this heading") ProMod3 is a modelling engine based on the [OpenStructure](https://openstructure.org) [[biasini2013]](references/#biasini2013) computational structural biology framework that can perform all steps required to generate a protein model by homology. Its modular design aims at implementing flexible modelling pipelines and fast prototyping of novel algorithms.
