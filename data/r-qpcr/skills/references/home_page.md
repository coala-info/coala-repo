## qpcR: Modelling and Analysis of Real-Time PCR Data

Sigmoidal model fitting, optimal model selection and calculation of various features that are essential in the analysis of quantitative real-time polymerase chain reaction (qPCR), according to Ritz & Spiess (2008) <[doi:10.1093/bioinformatics/btn227](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btn227)> and Spiess et al. (2008) <[doi:10.1186/1471-2105-9-221](https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2105-9-221)>.
