![](ufcg/image/UFCG_banner_marv.svg)

UFCG is a suite of database and pipeline about **highly conserved** and **evolutionarily informative** set of fungal marker genes,
pre-defined with a qualified set of genome sequences from the species across the kingdom of Fungi.

[Learn more](ufcg/about)

### *Contents*

---

### Databases

* 61 marker genes
* 1,587 fungal species
* 10,984 qualified assemblies
