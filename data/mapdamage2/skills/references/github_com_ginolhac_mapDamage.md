Introduction Complete documentation, instructions, examples, screenshots and FAQ are available at this address . mapDamage2 is a computational framework written in Python3 and R , which tracks and quantifies DNA damage patterns among ancient DNA sequencing reads generated by Next-Generation Sequencing platforms. mapDamage was developed at the Centre for GeoGenetics by the Orlando Group .
