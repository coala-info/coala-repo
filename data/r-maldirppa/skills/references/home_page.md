## MALDIrppa: MALDI Mass Spectrometry Data Robust Pre-Processing and Analysis

Provides methods for quality control and robust pre-processing and analysis of MALDI mass spectrometry data (Palarea-Albaladejo et al. (2018) <[doi:10.1093/bioinformatics/btx628](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btx628)>).
